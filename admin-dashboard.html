Leads Reserved

Leads = NEW business inquiries from email.

This section is reserved for prospective clients who reach out for the first time — potential new business, not existing clients or active matters.

A lead qualifies when:
  • The sender is not already a client or contact
  • The message is a first-time inquiry about legal representation
  • It arrives via email (inbox), contact form, or referral
Not leads: existing clients, court notices, vendors, newsletters, or internal correspondence.

Jude will monitor the inbox and surface qualifying new-business emails here once the inbox-monitor pipeline is deployed.
