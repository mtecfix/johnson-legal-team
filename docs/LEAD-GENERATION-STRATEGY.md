## Free & Low-Cost Lead Sources — Ranked by ROI

### TIER 1: FREE — Highest Impact (Do Immediately)

#### 1. Google Business Profile (GBP)
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Why:** Controls whether JLT appears in the local 3-pack above all organic results. 90%+ of local-intent queries show the map pack first. Free.
- **Action Items:**
  - Verify/claim listing for Johnson Legal Team PLLC
  - Add ALL practice areas (Family Law, Juvenile, Probate/Estate, Criminal Defense, Personal Injury, Real Estate, Traffic)
  - Upload 10+ photos (office, team, signage)
  - Write a detailed business description with keywords
  - Add service areas: Wayne County, Oakland County, Metro Detroit
  - Enable messaging
  - Post weekly updates (legal tips, case results, community involvement)
  - Request reviews from past clients (most important ranking factor)
- **Expected leads:** 5-15/month once optimized

#### 2. Justia Lawyer Directory
- **Cost:** FREE (basic profile)
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Why:** Domain rating 91 — one of the highest authority legal sites. Free profile gives a powerful backlink + direct client inquiries.
- **Action Items:**
  - Go to https://www.justia.com/lawyers — claim/create profile
  - Add all practice areas, jurisdictions, bar admissions
  - Write detailed biography
  - Link back to JLT website
- **Expected leads:** 2-5/month

#### 3. Avvo
- **Cost:** FREE (basic profile)
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Why:** Domain rating 89. Consumer-facing — people search here when they need a lawyer. Q&A feature drives leads.
- **Action Items:**
  - Claim profile at https://www.avvo.com
  - Complete all profile sections (education, experience, awards)
  - Answer 5-10 legal questions per week in relevant practice areas
  - Request client endorsements and peer endorsements
  - Avvo rating improves with activity (aim for 8.0+)
- **Expected leads:** 3-8/month (Q&A answers drive significant traffic)

#### 4. Nextdoor Business Page
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Why:** Hyper-local social platform. Neighbors recommend businesses. Extremely high trust factor. Perfect for Southfield/Birmingham/Oak Park area.
- **Action Items:**
  - Create business page on Nextdoor
  - Engage in neighborhood posts (answer legal questions when appropriate)
  - Post community involvement, office hours, free consultation offers
  - Neighbors will recommend JLT in threads asking "anyone know a good lawyer?"
- **Expected leads:** 2-5/month

#### 5. Facebook/Instagram (Organic)
- **Cost:** FREE
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐
- **Action Items:**
  - Maintain active business page
  - Share legal tips, expungement success stories (anonymized), community events
  - Join local community groups (Detroit, Southfield, Oak Park)
  - Go Live periodically for Q&A sessions
- **Expected leads:** 1-3/month

### TIER 2: LOW COST — High Impact ($100-300/year)

#### 6. State Bar of Michigan Lawyer Referral Service (LRS)
- **Cost:** $150/year (base) + $25 per additional practice area
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Why:** People call (800) 968-0738 looking for a lawyer. LRS matches them to panel attorneys by practice area + location. These are HIGH intent leads.
- **Action Items:**
  - Apply at michbar.org/programs/lawyerreferral_panel
  - Requirements: Active MI bar member, malpractice insurance, enhanced SBM profile
  - Select practice areas: Family Law, Criminal Defense, Probate/Estate, Personal Injury
  - Select judicial circuits: Wayne County (3rd Circuit), Oakland County (6th Circuit)
- **Expected leads:** 5-10/month (pre-qualified, high intent)
- **ROI:** One converted case easily covers years of membership

#### 7. Oakland County Bar Association
- **Cost:** ~$100-200/year
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Why:** Referrals from other attorneys who don't practice in JLT's areas. Lawyers refer to lawyers they know.
- **Action Items:**
  - Join and attend networking events
  - Get listed in referral directory
  - Build relationships with attorneys in non-competing practice areas

#### 8. Wayne County Criminal Defense Bar Association

### TIER 3: PAID — Highest Conversion (When Budget Allows)

#### 12. Google Local Services Ads (LSA)
- **Cost:** $50-150 per lead (pay only when someone calls/messages)
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Why:** "Google Screened" badge builds instant trust. Appears ABOVE regular ads and organic results. Only pay for actual leads.
- **Action Items:**
  - Apply for Google Screened verification
  - Set weekly budget ($200-500/week recommended)
  - Select practice areas with highest case value (Personal Injury, Criminal Defense)
  - Dispute bad leads for refund
- **Expected leads:** 10-30/month at $200/week budget
- **Best for:** Personal Injury (high case value justifies cost)

#### 13. Google Ads (Search)
- **Cost:** $5-50 per click depending on practice area
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐⭐
- **Action Items:**
  - Target long-tail keywords: "expungement lawyer Detroit", "probate attorney Oakland County"
  - Set geographic targeting: 25-mile radius around office
  - Use call extensions

#### 14. Facebook/Instagram Ads
- **Cost:** $5-20 per lead
- **Lead Quality:** ⭐⭐⭐
- **Best for:** Expungements (broad appeal), Estate Planning (educational content)
- **Action Items:**
  - Lead form ads targeting Wayne/Oakland County residents
  - Retarget website visitors
