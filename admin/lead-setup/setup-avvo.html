4

Request Peer Endorsements

Ask 3-5 attorney colleagues to endorse you on Avvo. Each endorsement raises your rating. Send them this message:

Hi [Name], I'm updating my Avvo profile and would really appreciate a peer endorsement from you. It only takes about 2 minutes. If you're willing, here's how: 1. Go to my Avvo profile: [your Avvo profile URL] 2. Click "Endorse this lawyer" 3. Write a brief endorsement (even 1-2 sentences is great) I'm happy to return the favor if you have an Avvo profile too. Thanks! - Attorney Johnson
Target attorneys you've worked with, opposing counsel who respect your work, or colleagues from bar association events.
5

Request Client Reviews

Ask 3-5 past satisfied clients to leave an Avvo review. Send this message via email or text:

Hi [Client Name], I hope all is well since we wrapped up your case. If you were satisfied with the representation you received, I'd be grateful if you could leave a brief review on my Avvo profile. It helps other people in similar situations find our firm. Here's the link: [your Avvo profile URL] Just click "Write a Review" and share your experience. Thank you for your time, and don't hesitate to reach out if you ever need anything in the future. Best regards, Attorney Johnson Johnson Legal Team, PLLC (313) 355-2216
Never offer anything in exchange for a review (no discounts, no incentives). Keep it a genuine ask.

Go to Avvo Q&A

How to do it:

Example Answer Template:

7

Understanding Your Avvo Rating

Your Avvo rating (1-10) is calculated from your profile completeness, experience, endorsements, reviews, and activity:

  • 1.0-3.9: "Attention" - No claimed profile, no activity
  • 4.0-5.9: "Concerned" - Incomplete profile
  • 6.0-6.9: "Good" - Basic profile completed
  • 7.0-7.9: "Very Good" - Complete profile + some endorsements
  • 8.0-8.9: "Excellent" - Full profile + endorsements + reviews
  • 9.0-10.0: "Superb" - Everything above + active Q&A + many reviews

Goal: Reach 8.0+ within 30 days by completing all sections, getting 3+ endorsements, 3+ reviews, and answering Q&A weekly.
