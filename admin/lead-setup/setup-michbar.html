Step 5: Michigan State Bar LRS

State Bar of Michigan LRS $150/year

Setup time: 30-60 min | Approval: 1-2 weeks | Expected leads: 5-10/month (HIGH INTENT)

1

Confirm Your Eligibility

You must meet ALL of these requirements:

If you don't have malpractice insurance, you'll need to obtain it before applying. This is a strict requirement.
2

Set Up Your Enhanced SBM Member Directory Profile

Before applying for LRS, your SBM profile must be "enhanced" (detailed):

Go to michbar.org
3

Apply for the LRS Panel

Submit your application online or by phone:

Apply Online at michbar.org
Or contact directly: Panel Coordinator: Monique Smith Phone: (517) 346-6323 Email: msmith@michbar.org
Calling Monique directly is often the fastest way to get set up. She can walk you through the entire process over the phone.
4

Select Practice Areas (4 included in base fee)

Choose your top 4 practice areas for referrals. Additional areas cost $25 each:

Optional add-ons ($25 each): Traffic, Juvenile, Real Estate, General Civil

5

Select Judicial Circuits (2 included in base fee)

Choose the circuits where you want to receive referrals:

Additional circuits: $25 each, or $300 for ALL Michigan judicial circuits.

6

Pay Registration Fee

The base fee is $150/year and includes 4 practice areas + 2 judicial circuits.

Total cost breakdown:

Base fee: $150 (4 practice areas + 2 circuits) Extra practice areas: $25 each Extra circuits: $25 each (or $300 for all MI) Recommended total: $150 (base covers your main areas)
7

How Referrals Work (Once Approved)

Here's what happens after your application is approved:

Person needs a lawyer
↓
Calls (800) 968-0738 (Mon-Fri, 8:30-4:45)
↓
Trained referral assistant asks about their case
↓
Caller pays $25 for a consultation
↓
LRS matches them to YOU by practice area + location
↓
You receive the referral (email/phone)
↓
You provide a 25-minute consultation
↓
If they hire you: set your own fees from there
ROI Reality Check: One converted case from LRS easily covers 10+ years of membership ($150/year). These are the highest-intent leads available — people who are actively paying money to find a lawyer RIGHT NOW. They've already committed $25 and talked to a human about their legal problem.
