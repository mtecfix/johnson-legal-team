Step 1: Google Business Profile

Google Business Profile FREE

Setup time: 15-20 minutes | Verification: 3-7 days | Expected leads: 5-15/month

1

Sign In to Google Business

Open the link below and sign in with the firm's Google account (johnsonlegalteam@gmail.com).

Open Google Business
If you're already signed into a different Google account, click your profile icon (top right) and switch to johnsonlegalteam@gmail.com first.
2

Search for Your Business

Google may already have a listing for you from public records. Search for:

Johnson Legal Team

If you find your listing: Click "Claim this business" and follow the prompts.
If no listing exists: Click "Add your business to Google" and continue to Step 3.

3

Enter Business Information

Fill in the following exactly as shown:

Business name: Johnson Legal Team, PLLC
Primary category: Attorney
Phone: (313) 355-2216
Website: [your website URL]
The business name must match your actual legal business name exactly. Don't add extra keywords (Google can suspend you for that).
4

Add Additional Categories

After selecting "Attorney" as your primary category, click "Add another category" and add each of these:

You can add up to 10 categories. These help Google show you in relevant searches.
5

Set Service Area

If clients come to your office, add your address. Then add service areas:

If you don't have a public-facing office, select "I deliver goods and services to my customers" and add the counties above.

6

Verify Your Business

Google needs to confirm you're the real owner. They'll offer one or more options:

Select whichever option is available to you. Phone is fastest. Once you receive your code, enter it at business.google.com to complete verification.

While waiting for verification, you can still edit your profile — but it won't appear publicly until verified.
7

Optimize Your Profile (After Verification)

Once verified, complete these for maximum visibility:

Business Description (copy and paste this):

Johnson Legal Team, PLLC provides experienced legal representation in Wayne and Oakland County, Michigan. Our practice areas include criminal defense, expungements, family law, child custody, divorce, probate and estate planning, personal injury, and traffic violations. Serving Detroit, Southfield, Birmingham, Pontiac, Oak Park, Madison Heights, and surrounding Metro Detroit communities. Free initial consultations available. Call (313) 355-2216.

8

Create Your First Google Post

Go to your Business Profile dashboard > "Add update" or "Create post". Copy and paste:

Now accepting new clients! Johnson Legal Team provides free consultations for criminal defense, family law, estate planning, personal injury, and expungements. Serving Wayne and Oakland County. Call (313) 355-2216 to schedule your consultation today.
Post once per week going forward. Ideas: legal tips, Michigan law updates, community involvement, "Did you know?" facts about expungement eligibility.
