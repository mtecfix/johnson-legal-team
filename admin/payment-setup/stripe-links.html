Create Payment Links

Time: 2 minutes per link | Share via: text, email, or website

1

Open Payment Links

Go to your Stripe Dashboard:

Open Payment Links

Or from dashboard: Payments > Payment Links in the left sidebar.

2

Click "+ New" to Create a Link

Click the "+ New" button. You'll set up what the client is paying for.

3

Configure the Payment

Choose one of these setups based on what you need:

Fixed Amount (e.g., Consultation Fee)

Select "Products or subscriptions" > Create new product > Name: "Legal Consultation" > Price: $250 (one-time)

Custom Amount (Client Chooses)

Select "Customers choose what to pay" > Set minimum (e.g., $100) > Label: "Payment to Johnson Legal Team"

Monthly Retainer

Select "Products or subscriptions" > Create new product > Name: "Monthly Retainer" > Price: $500/month (recurring)
