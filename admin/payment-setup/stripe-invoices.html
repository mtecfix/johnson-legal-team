Step 2: Send Invoices

Back to Hub

Send Your First Invoice

Time: 5 minutes per invoice | Client receives: professional email with "Pay" button

1

Open Stripe Invoicing

Go to your Stripe Dashboard and navigate to Invoicing:

Open Stripe Invoices

Or from your dashboard: Click Revenue > Invoices in the left sidebar.

2

Click "Create Invoice"

Click the "+ Create invoice" button (top right corner of the Invoices page).

3

Add Customer

Enter your client's email address. If they're a new customer, Stripe will create a record for them.

4

Add Line Items

Add what you're billing for. Examples:

You can add multiple line items per invoice. Add descriptions to each for clarity.

5

Set Due Date & Memo

6

Review & Send

Click "Review invoice" to preview what your client will see. Then click "Send invoice".

What the client receives: A professional email from Stripe (branded with your logo and colors from Step 1) with a clear "Pay this invoice" button. They click it, enter their card, and pay. Done.
7

Track & Follow Up

After sending, you can see the invoice status in your Stripe dashboard:

Stripe automatically sends reminder emails for past-due invoices. You can also manually resend or void invoices.

Tip: Enable "Smart retries" in Settings > Invoices to automatically remind clients before the due date.
